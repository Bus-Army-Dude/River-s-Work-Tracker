Careers at Sayville Public Schools Roleplay

We are always looking for passionate and dedicated individuals to join our community. Whether you want to be a bus driver, mechanic, dispatcher, or another key role, there’s a place for you here!

Current Openings

  • Full-Time Bus Driver

    Role Overview: Full-time bus drivers are responsible for safely transporting students to and from school. You’ll be assigned a specific route and expected to maintain the safety and discipline of passengers.

    Requirements:

    • Must be at least 13 years or older
    • Must have a mic
    • Must have a decent computer
    • Must have Discord and Roblox

    Apply Now: If you’re interested in becoming a full-time bus driver, fill out the application below!

  • Substitute Bus Driver

    Role Overview: Substitute drivers fill in when a full-time driver is unavailable. You’ll be responsible for covering routes and ensuring students are transported safely.

    Requirements:

    • Must be at least 13 years or older
    • Must have a mic
    • Must have a decent computer
    • Must have Discord and Roblox

    Apply Now: Submit your application below if you are interested in this role!

  • Mechanic

    Role Overview: Mechanics are responsible for maintaining and repairing buses. You'll ensure that all vehicles are in safe, working condition before each trip.

    Requirements:

    • Must be at least 13 years or older
    • Must have a mic
    • Must have a decent computer
    • Must have Discord and Roblox

    Apply Now: Use the form below to submit your application!

  • Dispatcher

    Role Overview: Dispatchers manage bus routes, schedules, and communication with drivers. You’ll ensure everything runs smoothly and on time.

    Requirements:

    • Must be at least 13 years or older
    • Must have a mic
    • Must have a decent computer
    • Must have Discord and Roblox

    Apply Now: Apply using the form below to be considered for the dispatcher role.
