Join Sayville Public Schools Roleplay Community!

We are always looking for dedicated roleplayers to fill various roles at Sayville Public Schools! Whether you're a bus driver, mechanic, or dispatch, there’s a position for you. Explore the available jobs below and apply today!

Available Positions

Mechanic

Maintain and repair the school buses to ensure they are running smoothly for all students. Work with the bus drivers to make sure everything is in top condition.

Full-Time Bus Driver

Drive the school bus full-time, picking up students, ensuring safety on the road, and making sure all routes are followed as scheduled.

Sub Driver

Serve as a substitute bus driver when needed. Step in for full-time drivers on their off days or during their absence, ensuring students still get to school safely.

Dispatch

Manage bus schedules, routes, and communication between the bus drivers. Ensure that everything is organized and runs smoothly during the school day.

How to Apply

To apply for any of the positions listed above, simply send a message to the principal or the head administrator in Roblox!

Alternatively, you can roleplay your way into a job by showing up in the appropriate area (e.g., wait at the bus stop if you want to become a bus driver or visit the garage to apply for mechanic).

Job Requirements

  • Mechanic: Must have basic knowledge of bus maintenance and troubleshooting.
  • Full-Time Bus Driver: Must have experience driving and a strong focus on student safety.
  • Sub Driver: Must be flexible and available to drive as needed, with experience in driving school buses.
  • Dispatch: Must have strong organizational and communication skills.

Perks and Benefits

  • Mechanics get to keep the buses in top condition and work with different vehicles!
  • Full-time bus drivers have consistent schedules and direct interaction with the students!
  • Sub drivers get flexibility and experience with different routes!
  • Dispatch gets to manage the entire bus schedule and ensure everything runs smoothly!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I switch roles later?
A: Yes! You can switch roles, but make sure to inform your fellow roleplayers.

Q: Do I have to be a student before becoming a bus driver?
A: No, but it’s recommended to have some roleplay experience first!
